Description:  
The task was to find 5 word pairs and explain their semantic similarity—how closely related their meanings are.

Activity:

Selected 5 word pairs.

Compared their meanings and contexts.

Explained similarities and differences.

Examples of Word Pairs:

King – Queen → High similarity (both royalty), difference in gender.

Car – Bus → Both vehicles for transport, differ in size and capacity.

Dog – Cat → Both pets, differ in species and behavior.

Pen – Pencil → Both writing tools, differ in material and permanence.

Apple – Mango → Both fruits, differ in taste and appearance.

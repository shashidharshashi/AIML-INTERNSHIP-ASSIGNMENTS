Description:  
The task was to write prompts for a resume, business idea, and study plan, then compare weak vs strong prompts to observe differences in AI responses.

Activity:

Designed weak prompts (short, vague, lacking detail).

Designed strong prompts (clear, specific, structured).

Tested both sets of prompts and compared outputs.

Examples:

Resume Prompt

Weak: “Write a resume.” → Output: Generic, incomplete.

Strong: “Write a professional resume for a Computer Science graduate with skills in Python, ML, and internships at IT companies.” → Output: Detailed, tailored resume.

Business Idea Prompt

Weak: “Give me a business idea.” → Output: Random, broad suggestion.

Strong: “Suggest a scalable business idea in EdTech for Indian college students, focusing on affordable online learning tools.” → Output: Specific, practical idea.

Study Plan Prompt

Weak: “Make a study plan.” → Output: Basic schedule.

Strong: “Create a 4‑week study plan for Data Science, including daily topics, practice exercises, and weekend revision.” → Output: Structured, actionable plan.

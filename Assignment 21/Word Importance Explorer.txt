#Use TF-IDF on 5 documents and identify top keywords with explanation.

Description:  
The task was to apply TF‑IDF (Term Frequency – Inverse Document Frequency) on 5 documents and identify the top keywords, then explain their importance.

Activity:

Collected 5 sample documents (short articles or text passages).

Applied TF‑IDF to compute word importance.

Extracted top keywords from each document.

Explained why those words are significant.

Example (Conceptual):

Document 1 (Sports): Keywords → “match,” “goal,” “team.”

Document 2 (Technology): Keywords → “AI,” “data,” “model.”

Document 3 (Health): Keywords → “exercise,” “diet,” “patient.”

Document 4 (Shopping): Keywords → “discount,” “offer,” “customer.”

Document 5 (Education): Keywords → “student,” “exam,” “learning.”

Explanation:

TF‑IDF increases the importance of words that occur frequently in one document but rarely across others.

For example, “goal” is common in sports but rare in other domains, so it becomes a strong keyword.

This helps identify the unique theme of each document.

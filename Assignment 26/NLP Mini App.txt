Description:  
The task was to design a small NLP application such as a chatbot, fake news detector, or keyword extractor.

Activity:

Explored three possible mini‑apps:

Chatbot: Simulates conversation by recognizing user intent and generating responses.

Fake News Detector: Classifies text as reliable or misleading using machine learning.

Keyword Extractor: Identifies important terms in documents using TF‑IDF or other methods.

Selected one prototype idea and explained its workflow conceptually.

Tested the design with sample inputs to check feasibility.

Learnings:

Understood how NLP techniques can be applied to real‑world problems.

Learned the difference between classification (fake news), extraction (keywords), and generation (chatbot).

Gained clarity on the importance of preprocessing (tokenization, stopword removal).

Blockers / Risks:

Small datasets reduce accuracy in fake news detection.

Chatbots require large training data for natural responses.

Keyword extraction may miss context‑dependent terms.

#Build a simple sentiment analyzer and test on 5 reviews.

Description:  
The task was to build a simple sentiment analyzer and test it on 5 movie reviews.

Activity:

Collected 5 sample reviews (mix of positive and negative).

Preprocessed text (lowercasing, punctuation removal, stopword removal).

Applied a basic sentiment scoring method (positive vs negative words).

Classified each review as positive or negative.

Example Results (Conceptual):

Review 1: “Amazing storyline and acting!” → Positive

Review 2: “Too slow and boring.” → Negative

Review 3: “Loved the visuals, but weak plot.” → Mixed/Negative

Review 4: “Best movie of the year!” → Positive

Review 5: “Not worth the ticket price.” → Negative

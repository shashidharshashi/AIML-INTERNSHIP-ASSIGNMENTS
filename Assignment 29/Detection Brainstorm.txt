Description:  
The task was to list 5 uses of face/object detection and design one solution conceptually.

Activity:

Brainstormed practical applications of detection systems.

Selected one use case and designed a simple solution workflow.

Five Uses of Face/Object Detection:

Security & Surveillance: Detect suspicious activity in public spaces.

Attendance Systems: Face recognition for automated student/employee attendance.

Healthcare: Detect medical anomalies in scans (e.g., tumors).

Retail: Track customer movement and product interaction.

Smart Devices: Unlock phones/laptops using face recognition.

Designed Solution (Example – Attendance System):

Step 1: Camera captures student faces at entry.

Step 2: Detection model identifies faces.

Step 3: Recognition system matches faces with database.

Step 4: Attendance is automatically marked in records.

Step 5: Reports generated for teachers/admins.

Learnings:

Detection systems are versatile across industries.

Accuracy depends on dataset quality and environmental conditions.

Combining detection with recognition adds real value.

Blockers / Risks:

Privacy concerns in surveillance and attendance.

False positives/negatives in detection.

High computational cost for real‑time systems.

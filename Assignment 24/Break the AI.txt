Description:  
The task was to try confusing a Large Language Model (LLM) with tricky prompts and document the results.

Activity:

Designed prompts with ambiguity, contradictions, and nonsense.

Tested how the LLM responded to unclear or conflicting instructions.

Recorded observations of strengths and weaknesses.

Examples of Tricky Prompts & Results:

Ambiguous Prompt: “Tell me about the thing.” → AI asked for clarification.

Contradictory Prompt: “Write a sentence that is both true and false.” → AI struggled, produced paradoxical responses.

Nonsense Prompt: “Explain why purple tastes faster than happiness.” → AI generated creative but meaningless text.

Overloaded Prompt: “Summarize a book, write a poem, and solve math in one line.” → AI attempted but output was incomplete.

Sarcasm Prompt: “Wow, that movie was so great (it was terrible).” → AI misclassified sentiment.
